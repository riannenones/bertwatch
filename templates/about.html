About the System

BERTwatch serves as a trigger warning classification system designed to help promote safer digital spaces by automatically identifying potentially sensitive content in user-generated text. It is built to recognize nine types of trigger warnings commonly found in online discussions:

  • Abuse
  • Dysmorphia
  • Death
  • PTSD
  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Suicide
  • Domestic Violence
  • Eating Disorder

The classifier analyzes input text and determines the three most relevant trigger warning labels which helps users, moderators, and researchers better manage sensitive content. Whether you're exploring content moderation, working in mental health, or simply concerned about digital well-being, this system supports responsible content handling and informed decision-making.
